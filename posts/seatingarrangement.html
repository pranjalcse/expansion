Seating Arrangement

Types of Seating Arrangements

  • Linear Arrangement: In a linear arrangement, the persons are arranged in a line. Usually, a single row of arrangement is formed.
  • Double Row Arrangement: In this type of arrangement, there are two groups of persons. One group is arranged in one row while the other group is arranged in another row. The persons in these rows usually face each other.
  • Circular Arrangement: In circular seating arrangement questions, the persons are arranged around a circular table, following certain conditions.
  • Rectangular Arrangement: These arrangements are similar to circular arrangements, with the only difference being that the persons are seated around a rectangular table.
